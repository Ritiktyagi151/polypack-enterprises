About Our Meal Trays

Even though meal trays sound like a small piece of equipment, a meal tray is a significant part of packaging when feeding people. Meal trays organize the serving and delivery of their meals in easy, neat, hygienic form. They provide portion control in eating, easy-to-carry convenience, and green packaging meant for takeaway services, catering businesses, and meal delivery services. Meal trays are designed for various compartments that help in keeping food items separate, fresh, and spill-proof.

Today's busy lives have transformed meal trays into a strong solution for ready-to-serve food in homes and offices, especially for restaurant, cloud-kitchen, and corporate catering services, at home and during travel. Lightweight construction and durability coupled with recyclability make such meal trays quite popular among commercial setups and consumers alike.

Meal Tray Types We Offer

2 CP Meal Tray with Lid

A tray with compartments that helps serve a combination of meals or snacks by keeping different food in each compartment. The attached lid keeps the meal fresh as well as ensures cleanliness as it is spill-proof during travelling.

3 CP Meal Tray With Lid

These types of trays are made up of three compartments, which make it quite easy to serve meals with rice, curry, or salad. The leak-proof mechanism keeps the quality and presentation of the food intact.

4 CP Meal Tray With Lid

A meal consists of four components to achieve balance among the main courses; this allows an establishment to offer main, side, and even dessert items in one tray. An extremely sturdy lid keeps the contents protected from spillovers and contamination.

5 CP Meal Tray With Lid

Most favorite with caterers and renowned food delivery services, this tray is best equipped to serve five different varieties of dishes without mingling their flavors.

6 Compartment Meal Tray with Lid

It ideally suits full-course meals, giving six separate compartments for starters, mains, and desserts separately, maintaining freshness.

8 Compartment Meal Tray with Lid

This tray is suitable for serving a large number of meals, allowing the business to serve mixed food combinations while being hygienic and presentable.

The Pasta Tray

This is a proper pasta tray, designed with a view for salads, continental dishes, and other pasta. This would be a sure spill-proof takeaway and delivery-friendly item for those taking food home.

Benefits of Meal Trays

Meal trays not only give a clean and organized packaging alternative but also keep foodstuffs fresh and uncontaminated. This compartment feature regulates portion control and avoids the mixing of dissimilar food items. The leak-proof lids facilitate spill-proof transportation, which is a key feature for delivery services. Meal trays are light, microwave-friendly, and easy to handle for customer convenience. Eco-friendly materials can also be used in making meal trays to support sustainable packaging practices, thereby enhancing their desirability to business owners.
