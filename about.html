Quality Processes

Quality Assurance (QA)

  • ISO Certification: We operate in accordance with ISO 9001 standards to ensure our packaging solutions consistently meet customer expectations and regulatory requirements.

  • End-to-End Inspection: From sourcing raw materials like food-grade paper or polymers to the final product, each stage undergoes detailed quality inspections.

  • Automated Precision Checks: Advanced moulding and packaging equipment ensure accuracy, consistency, and reduced human error during production.

Quality Control (QC)

  • Batch Sampling & Testing: We conduct systematic batch tests to verify strength, durability, leak resistance, and food safety compliance.

  • Defect Analysis: If any product issue arises, we perform root cause analysis and implement corrective actions to prevent recurrence.

  • Client-Driven Improvement: Customer feedback is actively collected and integrated into our quality systems to continuously enhance our product offerings.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

At Polypack Enterprises, our in-house lab uses advanced technology to ensure durable, hygienic, and high-performing packaging. We conduct rigorous tests across paper, plastic, and food-grade product lines.

  • Drop & Compression Testers: Simulate stacking and transportation stresses to verify structural integrity of boxes and containers.
  • Leak & Seal Integrity Testers: Ensure airtight and liquid-resistant seals for food-safe and chemical-safe containers.
  • Material Testing Equipment: Analyze tensile strength, impact resistance, and flexibility of raw materials used in production.

Testing Procedures

  • 1 Durability Testing: Ensuring packaging withstands handling, stacking, and transport stress.
  • 2 Leak & Seal Testing: Verifying airtight and secure sealing for containers and food-grade items.
  • 3 Thermal Resistance: Testing packaging performance under various temperature conditions.
  • 4 Material Safety: Assessing food-safety, hygiene, and compliance with regulatory standards.
