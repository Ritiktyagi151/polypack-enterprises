Types of Baking Trays We Have

1. Tulip Cup Molds

The unique petal-shaped design of tulip cup molds adds glamorous to baked goods. Molds of this shape are perfect for use in muffins and cupcakes to give a stylish finish to bakery items. The molds themselves are made of grease-proof, heat-resistant paper for simple effortless baking and serving.

2. Baking Paper Cup - Round

Round baking paper cups are perfect for cupcakes, muffins, and mini desserts. Their special features of being non-stick, grease-resistant, and heat-resistant make it easy to release and hold the shape of baked items. These paper cups are also said to bake conveniently and save grease on trays.

3. Polka Dots Cupcakes

Adding a fun and somewhat colorful twist to the baking process comes with the polka dots cupcake liners. These simple stylish cupcake cups are made for party events or even a bakery that just wants a little eye candy on its presentation. The polka dot cupcake liners are tough and food-safe and come in different colors; adding a bit of creativity to every batch of cupcakes.

4. Mini Plumpy

Super tiny liners for creating mini cupcakes, small bite-sized portions of food, or truffles are called mini plumpy baking cups. Their compact shape ensures uniform baking due to the strong construction, and their non-stick properties make serving free from hassle. This is one of the best choices for catering and bakeries, or even dessert businesses.
